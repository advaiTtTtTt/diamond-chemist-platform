यह document आपको बताएगा कि online orders में क्या-क्या situation आ सकती है,
आपको क्या करना है, और website + Razorpay अपने आप क्या कर लेगा।

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 1: CUSTOMER NE PAISA DIYA, DELIVERY NAHI HUI
(Customer ghar pe nahi tha ya address galat tha)
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Delivery boy gaya, lekin customer ghar pe nahi tha ya
   address nahi mila. Delivery nahi ho payi.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   1. Admin Panel kholo (website pe "Admin" button dabao)
   2. Us order ko dhundo
   3. "Cancelled" button dabao

🤖 WEBSITE + WHATSAPP APNE AAP KYA KAREGA:
   - Customer ko WhatsApp message jayega (Hindi mein):
     "Aapka order deliver nahi ho saka. ₹XXX 3-5 din
     mein wapas aa jayega."
   - Order ka status "Cancelled" ho jayega

💰 PAISA WAPAS KARNA (AAPKO KARNA HOGA):
   1. Razorpay Dashboard kholo:
      -> https://dashboard.razorpay.com
   2. "Payments" section mein order ka Payment ID dhundo
      (Admin Panel mein order card pe Payment ID dikhti hai)
   3. "Refund" button dabao -> Full Refund karo
   4. 3-5 din mein customer ke account mein wapas aa jayega

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 2: CUSTOMER NE PAISA DIYA, WEBSITE PAR "ORDER
FAIL" DIKHAYA (Internet cut gaya)
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Customer ne payment ki, lekin unka phone/internet band
   ho gaya. Unhe "Order Successful" nahi dikhaya.
   Ab wo phone karke poochh rahe hain.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   1. Admin Panel mein order dhundo
   2. Agar order wahan dikh raha hai aur status "Received"
      hai -> paisa aa gaya hai!
   3. Customer ko batao: "Aapka order mil gaya hai,
      chinta mat karo, dawa aa rahi hai."
   4. Agar order nahi dikh raha -> Razorpay Dashboard check
      karo. Agar wahan bhi nahi -> paisa nahi kata

🤖 WEBSITE APNE AAP KYA KAREGA:
   - Razorpay hamare server ko signal bhejta hai jab
     payment hoti hai, chahe customer ka internet band ho.
   - Order automatically "Received" ban jata hai.

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 3: PAISA AA GAYA, LEKIN DAWA STOCK MEIN NAHI HAI
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Customer ne 5 strips order ki, aapke paas sirf 2 hain.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   Option A — Partial Delivery + Partial Refund:
   1. 2 strips deliver karo
   2. Razorpay Dashboard mein jaao
   3. Us payment pe "Partial Refund" karo
      (baaki 3 strips ka paisa wapas karo)
   4. Customer ko WhatsApp karo bataao ki baaki paisa
      wapas aa raha hai

   Option B — Wait karo:
   1. Customer ko call karo
   2. Poochho kya kal tak wait kar sakte hain
   3. Kal stock aate hi deliver karo

🤖 WEBSITE KYA KAREGA:
   - Partial refund aap Razorpay Dashboard se manually
     karte hain. Website automatically nahi kar sakti.
   - Lekin order ka status aap "Accepted" rakh sakte hain
     jab tak deliver na ho.

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 4: PRESCRIPTION GALAT / EXPIRED / FAKE HAI
(Doctor ka parcha sahi nahi)
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Customer ne Schedule H1 dawa order ki (jaise sleeping
   pills ya strong antibiotics), aur jo prescription
   upload kiya wo expired, blur, ya fake hai.
   
   ⚠️ KANOON: Aisi dawa bina sahi prescription ke dena
   ILLEGAL hai. Kabhi mat karo.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   1. Admin Panel mein order ka prescription dekho
      ("View Prescription Image" link hai)
   2. Agar prescription sahi nahi -> "Rx Rejected" button
      dabao

🤖 WEBSITE + WHATSAPP APNE AAP KYA KAREGA:
   - Customer ko WhatsApp message jayega:
     "Aapka prescription valid nahi hai. Order cancel
     kar diya gaya. Paisa 3-5 din mein wapas aa jayega."

💰 PAISA WAPAS KARNA:
   - Razorpay Dashboard mein Full Refund karo
   - Payment ID order card pe dikhti hai

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 5: GALAT DAWA DI YA DABBE MEIN TAKKAR LAGI
(Wrong medicine ya damaged packaging)
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Customer ko galat dawa mili, ya glass syrup ki bottle
   toot gayi delivery ke dauran.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   - Agar AAPKI GALTI (wrong medicine): Free mein sahi
     dawa bhejo. Purani wapas mat lo (legal issue hoga)
   - Agar RAASTE MEIN TOOT GAYI: Free replacement bhejo.
     Insurance claim kar sakte ho agar lagatar ho raha ho.

🤖 WEBSITE KYA KAREGA:
   - Replacement ke liye aap manually ek new "₹0 order"
     system mein bana sakte ho taaki delivery boy ko
     address pata rahe. (Yeh feature baad mein add hoga)

--------------------------------------------------------
SITUATION 6: WEBSITE PAR DAAM AUR BOX KA MRP ALAG HAI
(Price mismatch)
--------------------------------------------------------

🔴 KYA HUA:
   Website pe dawa ₹100 thi. Customer ne ₹100 diye.
   Lekin naye batch ke box pe MRP ₹110 likha hai.

✅ AAPKO KYA KARNA HAI:
   - Is order ke liye ₹100 wali price HONOR karo.
     ₹10 ka ghata aap uthao — yeh customer ka trust
     banata hai.
   - Turant website ka price update karo:
     Admin Panel -> Products -> CSV upload karo updated
     price ke saath

🤖 WEBSITE KYA KAREGA:
   - CSV upload hone ke baad naye customers ko ₹110
     dikhega. Purana customer already sahi price pe tha.
